VRun is an interactive Endless Runner game made for Virtual Reality. This game is made for sportiv people!
Run on different tracks and try to reach the best score by collecting points and by avoiding obstacles with your real body movement.
Avoid obstacles by crouching, jumping or taking steps to the left or right with your body!
Dive into different surreal worlds by completing each given level and explore the adventure of VRun. 

Level 1

Have you ever been in space? No? We will give you the chance! Explore our first level and pass crystals and giant moving clocks in space. Watch out for sudden spikes standing out of the button and enjoy running between mountains.

Level 2

Do you ever imagine beeing in a dark lonely forest? What a scary feeling, right? Pay attention of toxic plants and mushrooms and try to avoid limbs appearing on the track.
If you are not feeling comfortable with this situation we strongly recommend you to run out of the forest for reaching the last and much friendlier world.

Level 3

Visit the Easter Island and see the impressive sculptures called Moai, that where made by the aboriginal people. Congratulations! If you are here, you reached the last level. Have you enough power to achive the best highscore or are you getting tired?
